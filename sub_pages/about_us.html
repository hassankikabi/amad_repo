ABOUT AMAD

AMAD which stands for “Ask Me About Dubai” is an informative platform whose aim is to avail general information particularly on promotions happening allover Dubai. In a city termed as “The Centre of Innovation” , Innovation comes from seamlessly sharing of information other than hoarding it. Here at AMAD we take pride in pointing you in the direction of the latest and biggest promotions on almost everything around town. We present to you the coolest leisure spots allover Dubai plus the all the upcoming events. Welcome aboard!

AMAD Strategy

  • Vision: To be the leading informer of the latest promotions allover Dubai.
  • Goal: To make promotional information readily available to everyone that needs it at zero cost.
  • Mission statement: To build long term relationships with our clients through providing exceptional services
  • Core value: We believe in serving our clients with integrity and honesty

Scope of Work

Our Service allows you to browse through various promotions available at different stores, you free to choose onto any as you wish, visit the specific website to gather more information , and also download copies of pdf E-catalogues without any restrictions.
